THE ALBUM

undressed at the symphony

A collection of quiet confessions, wrapped in delicate melodies and laid-back rhythms. Faye Webster’s soft, captivating voice guides you through a blend of folk, indie, and R&B, each track offering a glimpse into her vulnerability and introspection. With lyrics drawn from personal experience and universal emotions, the album reflects on love, loss, and life’s complexities. It’s the perfect soundtrack for those quiet moments when words fall short.

GIRL IN GUITAR

lost in melodies

Webster often bought a ticket to a performance at the last second. "Going to the symphony was almost like therapy for me,” she says. “I was quite literally underdressed at the symphony because I would just decide at the last moment that's what I wanted to do." Webster has never been more comfortable in her own skin than right now, which makes her ascent into the vanguard of young, independent artists even sweeter.
